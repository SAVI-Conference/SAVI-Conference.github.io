SAVI 2026 will take place in the [U Parkhotel](https://www.uparkhotel.nl/), conveniently located on the campus of the [University of Twente](https://www.utwente.nl/). The UPark hotel has rooms available for a reduced rate (including breakfast):

- comfort rooms for 135.50€ per room per night
- superior rooms for 145.50€ per room per night

Booking of these rooms is possible until **May 3, 2026** (as long as they are available) via the reception desk (bookings@uparkhotel.nl or [+31-53-433-1366](tel:+31534331366)). You will need a reference code for booking on of the available rooms at a reduced rate: GA001358.

If you want to stay somewhere else, we can also recommend the [Fletcher Hotel De Broeierd-Enschede](https://www.fletcherhotelenschede.nl/). But of course feel free to [search for other options](https://www.booking.com/searchresults.html?ss=University+of+Twente&src=landmark&dest_id=16569&dest_type=landmark&checkin=2026-07-29&checkout=2026-08-03&group_adults=1&no_rooms=1&group_children=0). Enschede is quite a small, walkable, city with great public transport. See [getting around]({{< relref "getting-around.md" >}}) for more information.
