The registration for SAVI 2026 has now been opened!

https://www.aanmelder.nl/savi2026/registration

The registration fee is 500 EUR. This includes coffee/tea during the whole event; lunch Mo−Fri; and a conference dinner. 

All corresponding authors who submitted an abstract have been notified about the acceptance of talks and posters. We were pleasantly surprised by the quality and quantity of submissions. In turn, we have a tiny worry of possibly reaching the venue capacity, so please register as soon as possible if you intend to come! Attendance is not conditional on having an accepted contribution; everyone is very welcome!

**Day pass **
It is possible to join - and pay - for one day only. We have a discount code for a day pass: DAYPASSAVI. When you register, you can enter this code and the final amount will change. You can also add the conference dinner (this will be held on Wednesday evening). If you opt for a day pass (of course you can also repeat this if you want to join for more days), please e-mail Rianne to let her know which day(s), so that she can adjust the order for the lunch.
